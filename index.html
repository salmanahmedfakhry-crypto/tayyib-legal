Common questions

How is content kept clean? Every upload is screened automatically and may be reviewed by a moderator before it goes public. You can also report or block anything in one tap.

How do I delete my account? Open the app → Settings → Delete account. This removes your profile and content.

Why does Tayyib ask for location? Only to calculate prayer times and the qibla direction. It's optional and you can turn it off any time.
